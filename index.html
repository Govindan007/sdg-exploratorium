About Climate Change

Climate change refers to long-term shifts in temperatures and weather patterns. While these shifts are natural, human activities have been the primary driver of climate change since the 1800s.

This section provides insights into how climate change impacts various ecosystems, human health, and the global economy. Through our interactive lessons, students will explore essential topics that include:

  • Climate Change Basics: Understand the fundamental concepts of climate change, including the greenhouse effect and global warming.
  • Renewable Energy Sources: Discover various renewable energy options, such as solar, wind, and hydro, and their role in reducing carbon emissions.
  • Climate Change Impacts: Examine the effects of climate change on weather patterns, sea levels, and extreme weather events.
  • Ecosystem Changes: Learn how climate change affects biodiversity, habitats, and the overall health of ecosystems.
  • Climate Action Solutions: Explore effective strategies and technologies aimed at mitigating climate change and fostering sustainable practices.
  • Sustainable Development Goals: Understand how the UN's Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs) relate to climate action and global sustainability efforts.

By engaging with these topics, students will gain a comprehensive understanding of climate change and the actions they can take to make a difference.
